# **🎬 SanVideoHub**

**SanVideoHub** is a sleek, completely private, distraction-free media and link collection manager. Built with a stunning UI inspired by Android 16 and Origin OS 6, it allows you to save, categorize, and watch YouTube videos, Instagram Reels, and web links—all in one centralized platform.

Say goodbye to YouTube distractions and cluttered bookmarks. With powerful features like a **Study Planner**, **Series/Playlist Mode**, and an encrypted **Private Vault**, SanVideoHub is the ultimate tool for learners, developers, and entertainment lovers.

## **✨ Key Features**

### **🎨 Stunning Origin OS / Android 16 UI**

* **Glassmorphism & OLED Dark Mode:** Deep blacks, translucent frosted-glass surfaces, and vibrant accent colors.  
* **Netflix-Style Hero Feed:** A massive, cinematic, edge-to-edge banner that highlights your most recently saved content.  
* **Responsive Design:** Works flawlessly on desktop and mobile with tailored layouts (like mobile bottom-sheet modals and floating action buttons).

### **📁 Ultimate Organization**

* **Smart Categories:** Sort items into Education, Software, Movies, Funny, Music, Tech, and Vlogs.  
* **Nested Folders:** Create custom folders (e.g., "Java", "Python") inside categories to keep topics perfectly organized.  
* **Universal Link Support:** Paste YouTube, Instagram, or any generic website link, and the app automatically fetches the correct player and thumbnails.

### **📚 Advanced Study Planner**

* **Automated Scheduling:** Select the skills you want to learn, input your daily free hours, and the app automatically generates a Pomodoro-style study schedule based on priority.  
* **Save/Load Templates:** Save your daily routines and load them up with a single click.  
* **Export to PDF:** Generate a clean, print-friendly Light Theme PDF report of your daily study schedule.

### **📺 Immersive Media Player**

* **Series / Playlist Mode:** Add multi-part courses. The player adapts to show a Netflix-style episode sidebar so you can seamlessly transition between parts.  
* **Smart Layouts:** Automatically detects vertical videos (like Instagram Reels) and adjusts the player to a portrait aspect ratio to prevent clipping.  
* **External Fallback:** If a YouTube video restricts embedding, a handy "Open in New Tab" button is available directly in the player.

### **🔒 Private Vault (Incognito Mode)**

* **Master Password:** Hide sensitive links and personal drive folders behind a secure password prompt.  
* **Auto-Lock:** Navigating away from the Private tab instantly locks it in the background to ensure your data stays safe.

### **💾 100% Private & Serverless Data Management**

* **Local Storage Engine:** Everything runs locally on your device. No servers, no accounts, and zero hosting costs.  
* **Backup Center:** Export your entire library to a JSON file or an Excel (CSV) spreadsheet.  
* **Restore:** Import your JSON backup on any device to instantly restore your library.

## **🛠️ Tech Stack**

This project is built purely with modern frontend technologies, requiring absolutely no backend or build steps:

* **HTML5** (Semantic structure)  
* **Tailwind CSS** (via CDN for rapid, responsive utility-class styling)  
* **Vanilla JavaScript** (ES6+)  
* **html2pdf.js** (For high-quality PDF generation)  
* **Phosphor Icons** (Sleek, consistent UI iconography)  
* **Google Fonts** (Outfit font family for geometric, modern typography)

## **📖 Usage Guide**

* **Adding a Link:** Click the "New Link" button. Choose between a Single Link or a Series/Playlist. Paste your URL, select a category and folder, and hit Save.  
* **Using the Study Planner:** Go to the "Education" tab and click "Create Schedule". Select your skills, set your hours, and click Generate. Use the "Download PDF" button to print your plan.  
* **Accessing the Private Vault:** Click on the "Private" category. Set up a master password on your first visit. Add your sensitive links here. Click "Lock" when you're done.  
* **Backing up Data:** Click "Backup Data" in the sidebar (or top menu). Choose "Full JSON" to download your database. To restore it on another computer, click "Select JSON File" in the same menu.
